## Cultural content

This project probes AI systems' depictions of the **Haida Nation** of Haida Gwaii, British Columbia.
Any generated depiction is an artifact of a machine-learning model. It is **not authored, endorsed,
or verified by the Haida Nation**, and it must not be treated as an authentic or authoritative
representation of Haida people, material culture, or artistic traditions (including formline art,
which carries specific cultural ownership). Generated images are retained locally for research
analysis and are deliberately **not published** in this repository. Do not redistribute, republish,
or present generated images as authentic Haida imagery.

Research grounding sources used by the sourced research mode are listed in `docs/sources.md` and
remain under their own rights.

If you are a member of the Haida Nation and want any part of this project changed or removed, please
open an issue.
