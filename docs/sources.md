# Grounding sources (sourced research mode)

When `research_mode: sourced` is enabled, the research and accuracy agents cite from this allowlist
of Haida-authored and reputable custodial sources, and record URLs and retrieved snippets in the
transcript. The Nation's own voice comes first.

- Council of the Haida Nation (official) - haidanation.ca
- Haida Gwaii Museum at Kay Llnagaay - haidagwaiimuseum.ca
- Bill Reid Gallery of Northwest Coast Art - billreidgallery.ca
- UBC Museum of Anthropology - moa.ubc.ca
- Canadian Museum of History - historymuseum.ca

Note: this project has not been reviewed or endorsed by the Council of the Haida Nation. See
`ETHICS.md`. Verify any land-acknowledgement wording with a Haida source before publishing.
